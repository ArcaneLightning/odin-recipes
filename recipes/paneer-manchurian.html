Paneer Manchurian

Paneer Manchurian is a delicious Indo-Chinese appetizer consisting of crisp fried Paneer (Indian cheese), in a slightly tangy & umami-rich Manchurian sauce.

Cubes of paneer are coated with a thick spiced batter and fried to perfection. Later these are dunked in a freshly made delicious manchurian sauce.

Though paneer can be shallow fried, air fried or baked, deep frying gives the best results of pillowy soft paneer with a super crisp coating.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of paneer (Indian Cottage Cheese)
  • 1/4 Cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons corn flour (corn starch)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • Water (as needed to make batter)
  • Oil

Ingredients for Making Manchurian Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon oil (olive or seasame)
  • 1/2 to 3/4 tablespoon garlic minced
  • 1/2 to 3/4 tablespoon ginger minced
  • 1/4 cup spring onion whites chopped
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup capsicum chopped
  • 1 teaspoon corn flour (corn starch)
  • 1/2 cup water (may need more)
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato sauce
  • 3/4 to 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon vinegar
  • Around 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons Spring Onion greens
